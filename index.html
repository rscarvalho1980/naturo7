Naruto Uzumaki

The protagonist of the series, Naruto, is a young ninja who dreams of becoming the Hokage, the leader of his village. He is known for his determined spirit and the desire to overcome obstacles.

Sasuke Uchiha

A teammate of Naruto, Sasuke is a talented ninja who seeks revenge against his older brother, Itachi. He is known for his Sharingan abilities.

Sakura Haruno

Another member of Naruto's team, Sakura, is a kunoichi (female ninja) with remarkable medical skills. She is infatuated with Sasuke.

Kakashi Sensei

The leader of Naruto's team, Kakashi, is an experienced ninja and one of the most enigmatic characters in the series. He is known for wearing a mask that covers half of his face.
